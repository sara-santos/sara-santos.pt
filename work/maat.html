MAAT TRANSVERSAL

GRAPHIC DESIGN
COMMUNICATION MATERIALS, VISUAL IDENTITY AND MERCHANDISING
©2020 ACADEMIC PROJECT

MAAT TRANSVERSAL is the name given to a fictitious program geographically dispersed throughout Belém, but under the auspices of the Museum of Art, Architecture and Technology. The program runs parallel to the permanent exhibitions and aims to make the museum and the Belém area more dynamic and to complement the long term exhibitions with other ephemeral events in a series of works in the area of plastic arts, performance music, installation, video, and mixed media. In this scope, it was proposed to develop an identity and graphics for the overall program and design specific pieces for the first month of the program, March 2020.
