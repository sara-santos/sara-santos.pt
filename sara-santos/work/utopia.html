UTOPIA MAGAZINE

MAGAZINE
ART DIRECTION, GRAPHIC AND EDITORIAL DESIGN
©2021 ACADEMIC PROJECT

Utopia is a biannual magazine about misplaced technology. This magazine seeks to embody the immaterial characteristics of technology in feelings, emotions and events of human life. It aims to explore a remote and undefined place where technology and personal emotions meet through different photography projects and other artistic areas. It intends to turn technology into something more personal and create an empathetic relationship with the reader, but at the same time introduce the work of important artists and photographers in the world of art and design. Each issue starts from a technological concept and relates to human emotions and feelings.


This is a magazine for those looking for an imaginary perspective on technology in our real world. The first theme is the shield. It is about feeling protected, comfortable and safe.
